Aura Living — 5 Logo Concepts

Designed to match the website's aesthetic · Warm minimalism · Editorial · Premium

Editorial Wordmark

CONCEPT 01
Pure typography. "Aura" in Playfair Display serif + thin gold rule + "LIVING" in tracked Inter caps. Magazine-style, timeless, works at any size. Best for: header, footer, packaging.

Arched Monogram

CONCEPT 02
"A" inside an arched frame. The arch suggests a mirror or doorway — a recurring home décor motif. Mark + wordmark side-by-side. Best for: header, business cards, app icon.

Circular Atelier Seal

CONCEPT 03
Atelier seal. "AURA LIVING" curving around the top + "EST. 2026 · LAHORE" curving around the bottom + central "A". Premium, packaging-ready, wax-seal aesthetic. Best for: packaging, labels, social avatar.

Botanical Branch

CONCEPT 04
Single line-drawn branch. Stem + 3 leaves — reflects the "plants & planters" category and the slow-living / artisanal aesthetic. Organic, warm. Best for: header, journal, Instagram.

Arch + Sun Minimal

CONCEPT 05
Arched doorway + gold sun. Suggests "home" + "warmth" + "considered living". Most minimal, most modern. The sun adds a quiet pop of brand gold. Best for: header, signage, app icon.
